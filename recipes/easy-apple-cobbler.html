Easy Apple Cobbler

You will need:

  • 6 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup water

  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Combine 3 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.

  2. Step 2

    Layer apples in an ungreased 9x13" baking pan, sprinkling each layer with cinnamon-sugar mix.

  3. Step 3

    Combine flour, 1 cup sugar, egg, and baking powder with a fork. Sprinkle over apples. Lightly sprinkle water over top.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown, about 30 minutes.

Consider adding some whipped cream to the top of the finished cobbler to add a good mix of flavors. If you want to try your hand at making some yourself rather than buying from a store, take a look at Easy Whipped Cream for a recipe that always takes the cake.
