Easy Whipped Cream

You Will Need:

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, chilled
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

  1. Step 1

    Combine the cream, sugar, sour cream and vanilla in a medium bowl. Use and electric mixer to whip to stiff (but not grainy) peaks.

  2. Step 2

    Chill until using, and serve cold. Use within one day.

Now that you've made this easy and delectable topping, try making a treat to add it to such as this Easy Apple Cobbler.
