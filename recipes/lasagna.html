Cheese Lasagna

You will need:

  • 1lb sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6oz) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

  1. Step 1

    In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

  2. Step 2

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

  3. Step 3

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13" baking dish. Arrange 6 noddles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta chees mixture. Top with a thrid of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan chees. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

  5. Step 5

    Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake and additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Now that you've made the original recipe, try adding different cheeses or meats to get something new next time.

Per Serving: 448 calories; protein 29.7g; carbohydrates 36.5g; fat 21.3g; cholesterol 81.8mg; sodium 1400.4mg.
